Build a cinematic, scroll-storytelling marketing website for **Nexvia** — a Portuguese B2B agency that sells automation and growth services to private health clinics (dentists, medical aesthetics, physio, psychologists). Nexvia also has its own product already live: **VetScribe**, an AI app for veterinary clinics.

---

## BRAND & TONE
- Dark, premium, clinical — dark navy/black background with electric cyan or cold white accents
- Professional but not cold. Confident. Results-driven.
- Typography: bold editorial headlines + clean body text
- Language: European Portuguese (Portugal)

---

## TECH REQUIREMENTS
- Single HTML file (vanilla JS + CSS, no frameworks)
- GSAP CDN for scroll animations and ScrollTrigger
- Lenis CDN for smooth scroll
- Fully responsive (mobile-first)
- Custom cursor (desktop)
- Smooth page load animation

## ANIMATIONS
- Hero: staggered text reveal on load
- All sections: fade-up on scroll enter (ScrollTrigger)
- Service cards: scale + fade in with stagger
- Pain points: appear one by one as user scrolls
- VetScribe card: special entrance animation — slides in from right with glow effect
- CTA section: parallax background effect
- Smooth cursor follow on desktop

## DESIGN DETAILS
- Color palette: #0A0A0F (background), #00C2FF (accent cyan), #4DFFB4 (accent green for VetScribe), #FFFFFF (text), #1A1A2E (cards)
- Border radius: 8–12px on cards
- Card style: glassmorphism with subtle border
- VetScribe card: slightly different accent (green #4DFFB4) to distinguish from main brand
- No stock photos — use geometric/abstract CSS shapes and gradient visuals
- All text in European Portuguese
